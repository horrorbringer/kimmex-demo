Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a quote take? expand_more

Once we have all the required project details, you can expect to receive a comprehensive quote within 3-5 business days. For larger, more complex projects, this timeline may be extended.

Do you handle permits and design? expand_more

Yes, we offer end-to-end services that include architectural design, structural engineering, and managing the entire permit application process to ensure your project complies with all local regulations.

Which areas does KIM MEX serve? expand_more

Our primary service area is Phnom Penh and the surrounding provinces. However, we are equipped to handle large-scale projects throughout Cambodia. Please contact us to discuss your project's specific location.

Can you work with my own architect's drawings? expand_more

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with external architects and designers. We can build according to your existing plans and will work closely with your team to bring your vision to life.

Do you offer free site visits and consultations? expand_more

Yes, we provide a free initial consultation and site visit to understand your requirements, assess the location, and provide preliminary advice. This helps us create a more accurate and detailed quotation for you.
